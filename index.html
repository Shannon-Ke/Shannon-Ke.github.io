ABOUT ME

Artist, Designer, Programmer
Georgia Institute of Technology 2020
Computer Science + Industrial Design

Hello! My name is Shannon Ke, and I am a university student pursuing degrees in both Computer Science and Industrial Design. At Georgia Tech, I spend my days attending classes, completing assignments, managing clubs, drawing and designing, playing a few instruments, and hanging out with friends. I am particularly interested in interacting with people and consumers through media and artificial intelligence in order to bring the greatest consumer satisfaction. On the creative side, I enjoy interacting with clients through art and design, receiving commissions from people from around the globe for different purposes, whether it be business logos or personal artwork.

Shannon Ke


Back at home in Atlanta, I worked to advocate for equality and justice for Asian Americans, partnering with organizations such as the Asian Americans Advancing Justice (AAAJ) and lobbying in the Georgia State Capitol for our rights. I helped to raise awareness of different cultures, starting clubs such as the Chinese Language Society (Walton branch) in order to expose students to ethnic cultures. On some occassions I have put together concerts for the elderly or disadvantaged, always happy to see their smiling faces when my friends and I play some uplifting and warm music for them. Otherwise, I spend my free time drawing and designing, taking commissions, coding my own games, or gaming with my friends.
